IMARA DAIMA PROJECT

Description

The project is located in Nairobi in the Imara Daima Estate neighborhood. This is about 10Kms from the Nairobi Central Business District and about two kilometers from Imara mall on Mombasa road. The neighborhood is well developed with maisonettes and apartments and is well served with all services and amenities. The Imara Daima Light rail station is within and other public transport is available. Electricity and water are on site.
