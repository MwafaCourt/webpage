ONE SYOKIMAU

Description

One Syokimau is a ultra modern development comprising of studio, one bedroom and two bedroom apartments. These Apartments combine comfort, convenience and luxury living making it the ultimate choice for your home. Each apartment is designed to benefit from breathtaking views of the surrounding.

Location

One Syokimau is located off Mombasa Road, 1KM from Gateway Mall, along Chady road, adjacent to Easy Hotel. In the vicinity of One Syokimau is JKIA- Jomo Kenyatta International Airport which is just a few minutes away, The Standard Gauge Railway (SGR) station, off Mombasa Road, plus it has easy access to East and Southern Bypass. Gateway Mall provides residents convenient plus an easy access to supermarkets, banks, restaurants, clinics and other services
